## G-stack office-hours synthesis

Brandon asked Timmy to complete the remaining G-stack exercise independently, choose the recommended options, and save the result back into the repository documentation.

Accepted synthesis:

- BrightSpeaker is a teacher pilot tool first and a school/district edtech platform second.
- Primary v1 user is the K-5 classroom teacher; student is the second user; principal/curriculum leader is the evaluator and expansion buyer.
- First real workflow is teacher creates class/code → students practice → teacher sees results → principal-ready pilot evidence.
- Product should prioritize class-code pilot backbone, privacy-minimized persistence, teacher progress view, K-5 activity library, and pilot evidence summary.
- Rule-based feedback remains the MVP default; LLM coaching stays deferred until teacher-reviewed.
- Parent/B2C, adult coaching, speech therapy, peer/multiplayer, and live teacher-student monitoring remain out of scope.
