# Bright Speaker — 30-day classroom pilot

**For teachers and coaches who want to try it with students, now.**

## What you get

- **One classroom** set up in Bright Speaker, up to 35 students.
- **6 speaking prompts** out of the box, spanning stories,
  descriptions, explanations, opinions, and book talks.
  Grade-band-appropriate prompts are being added through the
  pilot.
- **Student view**: 60-second webcam practice with live
  feedback on filler words, pacing, and eye contact, then a
  kid-appropriate results screen with encouragement and one
  concrete tip.
- **Teacher view** (lands mid-pilot): class list with last
  session, session count, and average score. CSV export for
  your gradebook or progress reports.
- **Private by design**: student video never leaves the
  device. We store transcripts and scores. We don't store
  video. We don't run ads. We don't sell data.
- **Google Workspace for Education sign-in** (rolling out
  through the pilot window).
- **A 20-minute onboarding call with Brandon** to set you up.

## What we ask

- Assign **2 practice sessions / week** for 30 days.
- A **20-minute wrap-up call** at day 30 to tell us what
  worked, what didn't, and what should change.
- Permission to anonymize your classroom's aggregate results
  for a case study. You choose what's included. You get the
  final say before anything publishes.

## What it costs

**Nothing.** Pilots are free. We're not selling yet.

When we publish commercial pricing, it will be designed for
building budgets — think "less than a subscription your school
already pays for," not enterprise SaaS.

## What we measure together

The three KRs we suggest for a 30-day pilot:

1. **Session completion rate.** Do students finish what they
   start?
2. **Filler-word trajectory.** Does the average filler-word
   count go down between first session and last session, per
   student?
3. **Teacher-reported confidence delta.** Short pre/post
   survey: do you see a student-confidence change in real
   speaking contexts (class discussion, presentations)?

We share the data with you at day 30 in a simple report.

## Starting the pilot

Reply to the email this came in, or write Brandon at

## The fine print

- Bright Speaker is a pre-launch K-12 tool. We're actively
  building teacher features through this pilot window.
- We do not make formal compliance claims (COPPA-compliant,
  FERPA-certified, SOC 2, etc.) because we have not yet
  completed those reviews. We will before any paid deployment.
- We will sign a school data privacy agreement if your district
  requires one to run a pilot. Email us and we'll work through
  the language.
- Everything above is promise-to-try. We'll write it down
  formally when you move from pilot to paid.
